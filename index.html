About

The Dehoney Center, in partnership with the Southern Baptist Theological Seminary, offers training in urban ministry and mission for every level of involvement and education.

Founded in 1859, The Southern Baptist Theological Seminary is the oldest of the seminaries of the Southern Baptist Convention. Enrolling more than 4,000 students annually, Southern Seminary ranks as one of the largest seminaries in the world.

The Southern Baptist Theological Seminary stands on the confessional vision articulated by the founding faculty in the Abstract of Principles and on the Baptist Faith and Message.
